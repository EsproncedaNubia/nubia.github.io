NFT Universe in Blockchain for International Artistss

NUBIA is a digital platform set in the diffusion of the international market of nonfungible works in the cultural offer, for artists and creatives who work with advanced technologies of Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), Artificial Intelligence (AI), Kinetic sensors per camera, 3D sound software and with other immersive and interactive technologies.

Multidisciplinary collaboration, teamwork and visionary anticipation of the future are the main drivers of this project. Nubia is a production of Lemongrass Communications SL through its cultural project Espronceda - Institute for Art and Culture; the center believes a lot in the strength of cooperation, either locally and/or internationally, This is why it has established important collaborations with Universities, Research Centers, Foundations and Institutes of Arts working in the field of technological innovation.
